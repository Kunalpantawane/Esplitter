Effective: June 2025 · Esplitter

1. Acceptance

By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old and agree to be bound by these Terms. If you do not agree, you must not use Esplitter.

2. Service Description

Esplitter is a personal and group expense-tracking application. It lets you record expenses, split bills with friends or groups, track personal budgets, and settle balances through UPI payment references. Esplitter is not a payment processor and does not move money on your behalf.

3. Account & UPI ID

  • You are responsible for keeping your password secure.
  • Your UPI ID is stored so group members can send you settlement payments directly via any UPI-enabled app. Esplitter does not initiate or verify these transfers.
  • Notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access to your account.

4. Data & Privacy

  • We collect only the information required to operate the service: your name, email, UPI ID, and expense data you enter.
  • Your expense data is synced to our servers to enable multi-device access and group collaboration.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • Expense data shared inside a group is visible to all members of that group.

5. Offline-First Behaviour

Esplitter stores data locally in your browser (IndexedDB) to work offline. Data entered offline is synced to the server when you reconnect. You are responsible for any discrepancies that arise if the same expense is entered on multiple devices before syncing.

6. Group Conduct

  • Do not add fraudulent or false expenses.
  • Do not use the platform to harass other group members.
  • Group admins may remove members and manage group settings.

7. Settlements

Settlement status (Pending → Paid → Confirmed) is tracked within the app for record-keeping only. Marking a settlement as paid does not constitute proof of actual payment. Disputes between group members are outside the scope of Esplitter's service.

8. Limitation of Liability

Esplitter is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. We are not liable for financial losses arising from use of the app, incorrect expense entries, missed settlements, or any reliance on data shown within the application.
